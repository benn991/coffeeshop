Our Menu

  • Americano

    A shot of espresso with hot water, perfect for those who prefer a milder taste.

  • Cappuccino

    A classic Italian coffee made with espresso, steamed milk and topped with foamed milk.

  • Latte

    A popular coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, often served with a thin layer of foam on top.

  • Espresso

    A strong coffee drink made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.

  • Mocha

    A delicious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream.
